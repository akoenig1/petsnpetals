ABOUT

Hi, I'm Jeanette! I offer attentive care for your pets and plants while you are away or if you're just in need of someone to provide a daily walk while you are out and about. I’m happy to come over and meet your pup and/or kitty so we can discuss personalized care.

I’ve had dogs and cats my whole life and truly think they help to make a house a home. I’ve provided pet-sitting assistance for years, worked the front desk at a vets office and volunteered at the Guide Dog Foundation for the Blind. Animals have long been a source of happiness for me and I've decided now is the perfect time in my life to make pet-sitting a part time business rather than just an occasional service to neighbors, friends and family.
